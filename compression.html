Compression

SMIL

“Compression starring Eddy and his Encoders!” is a presentation coded in SMIL. Providing a full lecture on the different types of compression, this project was my first real dive into coding.

The Design

The experience is targeted towards IMD students and aims to create an entertaining, nostalgic atmosphere with an 8-bit style and zany character. However the presentation is also well organized for note-taking by categorizing all information under different headings and sub-headings.

The Process

SMIL is very layout-focused.

The presentation is divided into different pieces or "regions". Each region acts as a container for the images on the slide. As the user navigates through the slideshow, different regions will change their images.
