Web Design, HTML & CSS

The Delish website was created for a fictional bakery called “Delish”. The site mockup has 4 pages coded (home, contact, about, products) as well as its very own logo.

As part of a Web Development class, this was my first website built from scratch.

The Design

Delish is meant to be a fun, warm and energetic brand. Their personality is reflected in the red colour scheme and unconventional titling.
